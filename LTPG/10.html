First ladders, now nets. We have a lot of weird metaphors



Sometimes (or rather often) capturing something means not attacking directly, but surrounding a bit from a distance.
It is very simple, (again? :D) but if you place your stones too close to your opponents, they may become a target. And while you are busy making sure your stones are safe, your opponent gets away.

But if you play just the right distance apart, your opponent will still not have enough space to make life, but you will have enough time to safely connect all your stones. Sounds simple? Test it out!
